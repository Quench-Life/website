## Our Mission

To become more like Jesus Christ while helping people grow through worship, discipleship, prayer, and authentic community.

## Our Values

Our values are anchored in Scripture and expressed through **Q.U.E.N.C.H. L.I.F.E.**

- **Q** — Quenching our thirst for God by becoming more like Christ.
- **U** — Unity in the body of Christ by focusing on what unites us.
- **E** — Engaging people with the love and compassion of Jesus.
- **N** — Nurturing strong relationships through small groups.
- **C** — Communicating in ways that strengthen progress and oneness.
- **H** — Humility because God honors it.
- **L** — Listening to one another as an act of value and respect.
- **I** — Instruction as lifelong learners in God’s Word.
- **F** — Forgiveness as an expression of God’s love.
- **E** — Edifying one another to strengthen the body of Christ.

## What We Believe

We focus on the essentials of Christian faith that unite us:

- God is one and eternally exists as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
- Jesus Christ is fully God and fully man, lived sinlessly, died for our sins, rose again, and will return.
- Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ, not by human effort.
- The Holy Spirit empowers believers for daily life and spiritual growth.
- The Bible is God’s Word and our final authority for faith and life.
